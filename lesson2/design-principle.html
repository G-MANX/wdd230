Design Principles

Nelson Azumah

Rule of Thirds




bbc.com

BBC world service news have the best design for Rules of Thirds. The image display fit the content of the story and makes readers view the visual aspect of the story first before reading the content.

Contrast




Amazon.com

The usability of the composition can make contrast lead the viewers attention. It helps the designers to emphasis the important attributes in the design.

Proximity




ebay.com

The elements are close together. They are near each other within the composition which is to be percieved as related items.The elements create relevancy, organization, structure and hierarchy.
