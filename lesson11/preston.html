Founder's Day Weekend!

by Done Day

OK. So there are no concerts this weekend. Or movies to go to. Or restaurant dining rooms to visit. Every day seems a lot like the next. But, you can have a good weekend in Boise and still follow the Stay at Home Order (and common sense). Here are some ideas: Walk your neighborhood. Getting out close to home is allowed under Gov. Brad Little’s order – and pretty good for you. Temperatures will near 60 this weekend – and a little time outside in your neighborhood can feel good. Wave at the neighbors. Notice the trees and the birds and the sky. Remember that things will get better. Chat with your neighbors. Speaking of your neighborhood, now’s a good time to have an over-the-fence chat. Or, for you apartment dwellers, from one patio or balcony to the next. Keep that six-feet-or-more social distance – but even a little human contact outside of your home can be a good thing. Do Something Good. Send a few bucks to a worthy charity, if you are able. Donate to the Idaho Foodbank. Or one of several other actions – check out our Do Something Good guide. Call your mom or dad (or both). When was the last time you checked in on your parents? Give them a call – or Facetime. Order a drink to go. If you happen to drink, there are a whole bunch of places that will deliver to you – beer, wine, even cocktails. Many of these are listed in our takeout guide. Better yet, enjoy it on your front porch. …Or some food. There are more than 100 local restaurants hoping to bring you some food or let you swing by to pick it up. You can help a local business, in the process.
